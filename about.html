About Us

This website intends to serve the community as a quick guide for most needed human services in our demography. Community Resource is an online portal that provides both easy access to new arrivals in the DMV area specializing in Virginia state and Washington DC.

This website specially helps those in the area seeking assistance finding human services, public and private agencies serving Northern Virginia.
